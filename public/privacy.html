Privacy at Plotwick

Last updated: July 23, 2026.

Plotwick stores the account information needed to sign you in, your credit balance, and the stories you save to your cloud library. Stories saved only on your device remain in that browser until you import them into an account.

AI generation

Your scenario, character, choices, and story history are sent to Plotwick’s AI provider to generate the next chapter. Do not include sensitive personal information in a story prompt.

Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe. Plotwick does not receive or store your full card number. It stores purchase identifiers and credit grants for fulfillment and support.

Sharing

A story is private unless you publish it. Published stories can be read by anyone with the link until you unpublish them.

Your controls

From the account menu you can export your Plotwick data or permanently delete your account. Account deletion removes cloud stories and published shares through database deletion rules.

Retention and security

Operational usage records are retained to protect the service, investigate failures, and understand generation costs. Plotwick uses access controls, row-level database security, and encrypted connections, but no online service can promise absolute security.

This policy should be reviewed by qualified counsel before a commercial launch and updated with the operator’s legal name, contact address, retention periods, subprocessors, and jurisdiction-specific disclosures.
